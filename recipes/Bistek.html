Description

This is a delicious filipino dish which is comprised of thin slices of beef and onions which is then stewed in soy sauce and lemon juice.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Black Pepper
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Simply combine soy sauce, calmansi juice, and black pepper with your beef and onions in a bowl. Marinate it for one hour.
  2. Pan fry the dish, but save the marinade on the side, don't include it on the pan.
  3. Serve!
